About Sherring

Sherring is committed to bringing the best out of people, facilitating you to use your talent, do what you love and make an impact. She walks the talk by pursuing her own passions. After ending her successful career in the higher education sector, she has become a full-time actress in 2019 and continues her coaching & training interests on the side, supporting people in chasing their dreams, and making meaningful impact on the world.
